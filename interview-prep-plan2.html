Decision questions

Your decision log (write 3 key decisions + why)
Goal: Never say 'we just used X.' Always say 'we chose X because Y, and the tradeoff was Z.'
L4
Failures, Bugs & Production Incidents
2 real incidents minimum · Root cause · Fix · Impact
▾
WHY THIS LAYER IS CRITICAL
Interviewers at senior level specifically probe failures. A candidate who says "everything went smoothly" is immediately distrusted. Failures show depth of understanding, ownership, and growth. Prepare 2 real incidents with specific technical root causes.
Incident structure (use this format)
WHAT broke — describe the symptom in user terms
WHEN — when was it discovered (prod vs testing)
ROOT CAUSE — the exact technical reason (not "a bug")
HOW you diagnosed it — tools, logs, reproduction steps
FIX — what code/architecture changed
IMPACT — users affected, downtime, revenue risk
PREVENTION — what you added to prevent recurrence
Likely incidents in your work
Razorpay webhook race condition (payment confirmed but UI stuck)
RBAC edge case — user saw unauthorized data after role change
useEffect fetching stale data on rapid filter changes
SSR hydration mismatch causing layout flash
Session expiry not handled — user lost form state mid-checkout
Slow dashboard query — N+1 problem discovered in prod
Document your 2 incidents
Goal: Tell each incident story in 2 minutes. Technical depth + personal ownership. Never blame teammates.
